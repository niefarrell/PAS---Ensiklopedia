East Alton Elementary School District #13

The East Alton Elementary School District #13 was consolidated and renumerated in 1956.
The Superintendent of East Alton Elementary School District #13 in 1956 was Charles T. Gabbert. Members of its Board of Education were:
Rhea G. Vroman
Birdine Groshong
Emory Walkington
Richard Welch
Rafael Marderosian
Orval C. Lay
