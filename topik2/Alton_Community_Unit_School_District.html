Alton Community Unit School District #11

The Alton Community Unit School District #11 was consolidated and renumerated in 1952.
The Superintendent of Alton Community Unit School District #11 in 1952 was Dr. J.B. Johnson. Members of its Board of Education were:
Dr. Robert B. Lynn
George L. Davis
Robert L. Goulding
Charles R. Freeman
Dr. Charles J. Hemphill
E.P. Waterhouse
Jerry Middleton
Today, the Alton Community Unit School District #11 is comprised of the following schools: Alton High School, Alton Middle School, East Elementary School, North Elementary School, West Elementary School, Lovejoy Elementary School, Lewis and Clark Elementary School, Gibson Brown Elementary School, Eunice Smith Elementary School, Early Childhood Center, and the Motivational Achievement Center.
